How we got here

A short timeline with dramatic (and slightly exaggerated) captions.

Meeting through matrimonial website
Our journey began online, and we decided to take a chance and meet in person. Little did we know this would be the start of something beautiful.
First date at Dyu Art Cafe, Bangalore
I brought flowers and my heart was racing wandering where to meet you. We spent the entire day together, even caught a movie at the theatre. You made me walk around exploring every corner with you.
Cubbon Park and movie shopping dates
We met a couple more times exploring the beautiful Cubbon Park, catching more movies together, and going shopping. Each moment with you became more special than the last.
Your home and homemade love
You invited me to your home and cooked me the most delicious food. In that moment, I saw your compassion, care, and the warmth you bring to everything you do.
Meeting your family
You came to my home with your family, and I knew this was something real. Your family welcomed me with open hearts, and I felt at home.
Our engagement
We got engaged recently, and everything felt perfect. When I knelt down and proposed, you told me you were so happy. That happiness made me the happiest man alive.
